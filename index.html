Want a friend to talk to?

Satsuki Bot is a chatbot created to hold a regular conversation with you in your discord server!

How to launch #SatsukiBot?

Follow these steps closely to successfully launch Satsuki in your discord server!

Step #1

Log in to Discord, invite #SatsukiBot into your server!

NOTE: Giving #SatsukiBot access to your server allows her to read and send messages in specific channels.

Step #2

Give permission to #SatsukiBot to take all your money >:) to send messages!

NOTE: Satsuki Bot will take around 10-20 seconds to load when first launched.

Step #3 🎉

Congrats! You’re finally at Step 3!
Say “hello” and that will start a conversation with Satsuki!

NOTE: Don’t say “father caramel” or any random letters/words, the bot WILL crash and send messages that comprise of “!!!!!!!” or “father is a liar”. The response is generated based on the user’s input and the script.
